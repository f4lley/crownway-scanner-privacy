Crownway Scanner

Privacy Policy & Terms of Service

Last updated: 17 march 2026

1. About the Application

Crownway Scanner is a mobile application developed by Falley B.V., designed for scanning and validating tickets, products, and access credentials for events and organizations.

---

2. Privacy Policy

2.1 User Account Data

Users log in using accounts created within the Crownway platform. We process:

  • Name
  • Login credentials (securely stored and encrypted)

This data is used solely for authentication and access to the correct organizations and workspaces.

---

2.2 Ticket Holder Data

The app may display data of individuals who have purchased tickets or products through the platform.

This may include:

  • Name
  • Ticket information
  • Purchased products or add-ons

This data:

  • Is not permanently stored on the device
  • Is only used for validation and access control
---

2.3 Camera Usage

The app uses the device camera and optionally the flashlight to scan QR codes.

No photos or videos are recorded, stored, or shared.

---

2.4 Offline Functionality

The app may function offline. In this case:

  • Scan results are temporarily stored on the device
  • Data is synchronized when an internet connection becomes available
---

2.5 Purpose of Data Processing

We process data solely for:

  • Event access control
  • Validation of tickets and products
  • Fraud prevention
  • Improving app functionality
---

2.6 Data Sharing

We do not sell personal data. Data is only shared:

  • With the organization using the platform
  • When required by law
---

2.7 Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect data.

---

2.8 Data Retention

Data is not retained longer than necessary for the purposes described above.

---

2.9 User Rights

Users may have the right to:

  • Access their data
  • Request correction
  • Request deletion

Requests can be sent to:
apps@falley.com
