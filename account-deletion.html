Account & Data Deletion

Crownway Scanner

Crownway Scanner is part of the Crownway platform, developed by Falley B.V. User accounts are created and managed by organization administrators through the Crownway dashboard. The mobile app itself does not support account registration.

How to Request Account Deletion

To request deletion of your account and associated data, please send an email to:

📧 apps@falley.com

Include the following information in your request:

  • Your full name
  • The email address associated with your account
  • The organization or workspace you are part of (if known)

Alternatively, you may request account deletion through your organization administrator via the Crownway dashboard.

---

What Data Will Be Deleted

Upon processing your request:

  • Your user account will be permanently deleted
  • Your access to all organizations and workspaces will be removed
  • Your personal data (such as name and email address) will be deleted
---

Data That May Be Retained

Some limited data may be retained where necessary for:

  • Legal obligations
  • Fraud prevention and security
  • Administrative or financial compliance

This data will be securely stored and deleted as soon as it is no longer required.

---

Processing Time

We aim to process all account deletion requests within 7 business days.

---

Important Information

  • Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone
  • You may lose access to tickets, products, and event-related data
---
